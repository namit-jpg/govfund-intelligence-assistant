# Product Brief

GovFund Intelligence Assistant is a production-oriented MVP for reviewing public campaign finance records from FEC and TEC sources.

It supports:

- Complete paginated OpenFEC ingestion.
- Official TEC CSV/XLSX import.
- Raw and normalized source-backed records.
- Employer/company signal analysis with caveats.
- Recipient and committee dossiers.
- Topic tagging for infrastructure-adjacent research.
- Exportable evidence, audit logs, and data quality flags.
- Grounded AI briefings only when OpenAI is configured.

The product avoids fake records, fake AI output, and legally risky claims.
